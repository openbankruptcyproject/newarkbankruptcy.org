Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13: Which Is Right for You?

Chapter 7 -- Fresh Start

Chapter 7 eliminates most unsecured debts in about 3-4 months. You must pass a means test.

  • Discharge in 3-4 months
  • Eliminates credit cards, medical debt
  • Must pass the means test
  • Filing fee: $338
Learn more about Chapter 7 →

Chapter 13 -- Payment Plan

Chapter 13 lets you keep your property while repaying debts over 3-5 years. No means test required.

  • 3-5 year repayment plan
  • Keep your home and car
  • Catch up on missed payments
  • Filing fee: $313

What to Expect When Filing

  1. Credit counseling -- Required course within 180 days before filing ($15-25).
  2. File your petition -- The automatic stay takes effect immediately.
  3. 341 meeting -- About 30 days after filing. Brief meeting of creditors (5-10 minutes).
  4. Debtor education -- Second required course before discharge ($10-25).
  5. Discharge -- Chapter 7: ~60 days after 341. Chapter 13: after 3-5 year plan.

How Much Does Bankruptcy Cost?

  • Chapter 13 filing fee: $313
