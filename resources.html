Legal Aid Organizations

Legal Services of New Jersey

Provides free civil legal services to low-income individuals, including bankruptcy representation.

  • Phone: (888) 576-5529
  • Website: lsnj.org

Northeast New Jersey Legal Services

  • Phone: (201) 487-2166
  • Website: nnjls.org

Credit Counseling and Debtor Education

Two courses are required: pre-filing credit counseling and post-filing debtor education. You must use agencies approved by the U.S. Trustee Program.

  • Approved agencies: justice.gov - approved agencies
  • Cost: $15-25 per course (reduced fees available)
  • Format: Online, phone, or in-person

U.S. Trustee Program

  • Region 3 (includes D.N.J.): (973) 645-3014
  • Website: justice.gov/ust

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the Newark bankruptcy court?

50 Walnut St, Newark, NJ 07102. Phone: (973) 645-4764.

Is there free legal help for bankruptcy in Newark?

Yes. Legal Services of New Jersey ((888) 576-5529) provides free services to qualifying individuals.

Where can I take the required credit counseling?

See the approved list at justice.gov/ust. Most courses are $15-25 online.
